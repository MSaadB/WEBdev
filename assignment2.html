Websites about Protected Areas Issues

As environmental concerns grow, and resource scarcity becomes more immediate, protected environmental areas play a growing vital role. It is not just about preserving our environment and biodiversity, but is related to our very survival as the human species. At our current nexus, it is crucial to understand the issues surrounding these areas and it is becoming more & more necessary to develop strategies for their conservation.
